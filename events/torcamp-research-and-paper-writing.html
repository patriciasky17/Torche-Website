TORCAMP Research & Paper Writing

TORCAMP (abbreviated TORCHE Bootcamp) is a TORCHE Bootcamp Series that aims to provide focused learning on several specific topics that can improve the skill set of participants. Torcamp Research and Paper Writing is an event for students and professionals who wants to write and publish scientific papers and journals, and students who wants to excel in paper competitions.

The event will be held in one week. In the first day, participants will be given materials how to write a scientific papers and journals. Then for 5 days, participants will try to write scientific papers with supervisions from selected mentors. In the final day, participants will present their papers and juried by 3 professionals. The best paper will be given special prize.

About the Speakers

Dr. Muhammad Arif Darmawan, S.Si., M.T

Known as “bang Arif”, is one of the tutors from Torche Education who provides teaching related to basic chemistry, organic chemistry, and analytical chemistry. He is an awardee of PMDSU (fast track master’s degree for 4 years) batch IV and a graduate of the doctoral program at the Department of Chemical Engineering, UI. Arif is currently pursuing a postdoctoral program at the Biorefinery Research Group (Kelris), the Center for Process and Manufacturing Industry Technology Research, the Energy and Manufacturing Research Organization, the National Research, and Innovation Agency (BRIN). He has a research topic on biorefinery and biomass valorization, especially in oil palm empty fruit bunches and tengkawang fat. He has 12 scientific publications on Scopus with 4 papers with Q1 quality and Scopus H-Index of 3. In addition, Bang Arif also has 1 book chapter published on Wiley with the title Coconut Oil.


Michael

Michael is currently a fifth semester student of the Department of Chemical Engineering, University of Indonesia. When he was in high school, he became the 5th winner of OSK as well as an OSP finalist in chemistry in 2019. In 2020, he was accepted as a Chemical Engineering student. Several awards were won during college, including being the gold winner of KN MIPA PT in the field of chemistry, which was held by the Ministry of Education and Culture, Research and Technology, 1st Place in the Chemical Engineering Competition, as well as other awards at the national and internal levels. He is also active in teaching, namely as a chemistry tutor at Dr. Ganesha Education (2020-2021) and assistant lecturer courses (2021-2022). Currently, he serves as the ON MIPA PT 2022 olympiad trainer in the field of chemistry at FMIPA UI and Chemistry Coordinator at PT Eduka Teknologi Indonesia.
